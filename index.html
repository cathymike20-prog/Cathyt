Neutrons

Electrically neutral (0 charge) and weighing ~1 amu. Located in the nucleus, they provide structural spacing, shielding protons' repulsion forces.

Dalton's Billiard Ball Model

John Dalton

Dalton conceptualized atoms as solid, indivisible spheres. He claimed that chemical reactions reorganize atoms, but atoms cannot be broken or created.

Scientific Insight

While incorrect about atoms being solid and indivisible, Dalton's model laid the foundation for modern stoichiometric calculations and quantitative chemistry laws.
